WeMove
A web3 dapp interacting with a smart contract on the SmartBCH Amber Testnet. For testing purposes only!
  • Type your name
  • Tap on the log button to store it on-chain
  • Tap on the call button to increment on-chain the number of times you've interacted with the smart contract
  • You can switch between different SmartBCH accounts in your wallet if your wallet supports this functonality. Make sure your wallet's network is set to the SmartBCH Amber Testnet.
  • Enjoy!
